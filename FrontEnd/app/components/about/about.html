Who are we ?
We are four students from an engineer school in France called Polytech Nice-Sophia.
What is FX Machine project ?
We have developed a web site for guitarists who would like to have their own effect pedals on their computers.
Tired to buy or test a lot of pedal because you can't find the perfect sound?
Don't worry, FX Machine is the solution made for you! Thanks to our system, you will be able to create your own pedal by selecting every components of your pedal.
Wait we made more functionalities!
Once you have built your pedal with all effects you need, you can save it and share it to your friends. In fact, when you save, your pedal is public and people can use it.
